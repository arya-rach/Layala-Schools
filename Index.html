Layala schools


























The Layala Schools is the college unit of Ateneo de Manila University, a private Catholic research university in Quezon City, Philippines, run by the Jesuits. It is a group of four schools that offer undergraduate and graduate degree programs in the arts and sciences. It is located at the university's Layala Heights campus in Quezon City. The Layala Schools offer Bachelor of Arts (AB), Bachelor of Science (BS), and Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA) degrees at the undergraduate level, and Master of Arts (MA), Master of Science (MS), and Doctorate degrees at the graduate level.
